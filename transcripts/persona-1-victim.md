# Persona 1: External / Entitled / Self-Focused

## Session Transcript

**Q: If you had to sum up your day in one word, what would it be?**  
A: Tough

**Q: When things didn’t go as expected, what was your first instinct?**  
A: Wait for someone to step in

**Q: Looking back now, was there a moment you had more choice than it felt like?**  
A: Not really

**Reflection:**  
Days like this can feel like things just happen to you. But even noticing where you might have had a choice is a shift.

---

**Q: Think of one interaction today. What stands out most?**  
A: I felt I deserved more recognition

**Q: Where was most of your attention during the day?**  
A: Whether I’d be recognized

**Reflection:**  
It’s natural to notice what’s missing. But shifting toward contribution can change how the system responds.

---

**Q: When you think about today’s biggest moment, who comes to mind first?**  
A: Just me

**Q: What was taking up most of your mental space?**  
A: My stress

**Reflection:**  
When everything feels personal, the weight increases. Expanding perspective can reduce that pressure.

---

## Summary Insight

The user showed:

- External locus of control
- Entitlement orientation
- Self-focused perspective

This resulted in reflections that gently redirect toward agency and contribution.
